Egusi soup
Ingredients

Egusi soup, also known as melon seed soup, is a healthy and easy dinner recipe that is sure to make your mouth water. Made with a variety of vegetables, this meal is enriched with nature's earthly goodness and will definitely leave you licking your fingers.

Preparation method

  1. Cut the meat or fish and season. Add water and boil until tender. Set aside.
  2. Mix the Egusi in water. Set aside. Blend the tomatoes, the second onion and the chili. Set aside
  3. Heat the palm oil in a pot and add the egusi mixture. Stir well and allow it to fry for a moment. Add the blend and stir well. Then add the ground crayfish, seasoning and stock fish.
  4. Add water and allow to cook for some minutes. Add boil the meat and stir. Turn the heat down and simmer for a further two minutes.
  5. Wash and finely slice the ugwu leaves. Add to the simmering soup and stir. Continue to simmer for some minutes to allow the vegetables to soften.
  6. Serve with cassava fufu or garri.

Pancake

Ingredients

Plain flour 150 naira
Milk 200 naira
Eggs 120 naira
Vegetable oil 200 naira
Sugar 100 naira
A pinch of salt

Pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.

Preparation method

  1. put the flour into a large bowl, make a well in the centre and pour in the milk and eggs. Give the liguid mixture a quick whisk before incorporating the flour. Add the sugar and a pinch of saltContinue to whisk until you have a smooth batter
  2. Add vegetable oil and whisk thoroughly.
  3. Take a frying pan, dip some kitchen roll in the pan an carefully wipe the inside of the pan. Heat the pan inside a medium heat for one minute.
  4. Add just under a ladleful of batter to the pa and immediately start swirling it round the pan to get a nice even layer.
  5. cook the pancake for 30-40 seconds. Use a palette knife to lift the pancake carefully to look at the underside to check it is golden-brown before turning over. Cook the other side for pproximately 30-40 seconds and transfer to a serving plate.

Egg sauce

Ingredients

Fresh tomatoes 200 naira
Pepper 50 naira
4 eggs 160 naira
Vegetable oil 200 naira
Onions 50 naira
Seasoning 100 naira
Salt to taste

Egg sauce is a very simple sauce made with eggs and tomatoes, mostly refferd to as tomato egg sauce.

Preparation method

  1. Slice tomatoes pepper and onions.
  2. Set your frying pan on heat and add vegetable oil, allow one minute before adding the sliced tomatoes, pepper and onions.
  3. Allow to fry for some minutes and stir always.
  4. Once it is adequately fried, add salt to taste. Add seasoning.
  5. Leave for a while and turn off the heat.
